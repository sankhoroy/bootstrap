Journalist-activist Gauri Lankesh gunned down outside her house

BENGALURU: Journalist, writer and activist Gauri Lankesh was shot dead outside her residence in Rajarajeshwari Nagar, southwest Bengaluru, on Tuesday night.

The coldblooded killing of the 55-year-old activist who took rightwing politics head-on in Karnataka brought back memories of the murders of Kannada writer-thinker MM Kalburgi in Dharwad in 2015, communist leader Govind Pansare the same year in Kolhapur, and rationalist Narendra Dabholkar in Pune in 2013.
